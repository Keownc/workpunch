Getting started

Workpunch is a work application for both the employer and employees to help increase productivity, through easy of use and efficiency. To help guide you through this transition we have provided a short tutorial to help you along the way or just follow the following instructions.

  • First register your account with your companyID provided by your respected companies.

  • Upon logging in, you will be greeted with profile section followed by your list of features.

  • The Timepunch feature has three avaible options, the punch system, an overtime calculator and log view to review the time you clocked In and Out each day.

  • The Sick Leave feature allows you to keep track of the days you were out sick and allows you to sent in your form without going into the offices.
